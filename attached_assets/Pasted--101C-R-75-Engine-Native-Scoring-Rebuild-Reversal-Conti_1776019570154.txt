# T001 — Rebuild r75_reversal_engine with native scoring
File:
- `artifacts/api-server/src/core/engines/r75ReversalEngine.ts`
(or the actual current R_75 reversal engine file in repo)

This is the PRIMARY R_75 setup family.

### Primary reversal idea
BUY or SELL when price is at a meaningful 30-day range extreme and shows directional reversal confirmation.

### Build a 6-component native score for reversal
Use R_75-specific component names and logic such as:

1. Range Extremity
- how close price is to the 30-day high or low
- stronger score the closer to the edge

2. Reversal Confirmation
- actual directional reversal evidence
- reversal candle quality
- directional shift quality
- no lagging generic fallback explanation

3. Stretch / Deviation Quality
- how stretched price is relative to EMA / local structure / z-score context
- enough displacement to justify reversal

4. Structure Quality
- whether the reversal is happening at a meaningful structural level, not random mid-range noise

5. Entry Efficiency
- how early/clean the entry is relative to the reversal point
- avoid very late entries

6. Expected Move Sufficiency
- whether there is enough runway back toward the opposite side / mean to justify a real swing trade

### Reversal engine gate
Add:
- `R75_REVERSAL_MIN_GATE = 55`

This is the PRIMARY R_75 engine gate.

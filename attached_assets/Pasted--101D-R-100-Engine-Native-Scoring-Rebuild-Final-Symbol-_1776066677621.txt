## T001 â€” Rebuild r100_reversal_engine with native scoring

This is the PRIMARY engine.

### Reversal thesis
Use reversal when price is at a meaningful 30-day range extreme and shows directional reversal confirmation.

This is the most important R_100 family.

### Build a 6-component native reversal score
Implement a real weighted score from 0â€“100 using these dimensions:

1. **Range Extremity** (25%)
- closeness to 30-day high or low
- stronger score nearer the structural boundary

2. **Reversal Confirmation** (22%)
- directional reversal candle evidence
- wick/body quality
- RSI support
- EMA slope turning / flattening / inflecting

3. **Stretch / Deviation Quality** (18%)
- z-score stretch
- Bollinger edge pressure
- EMA displacement quality

4. **Structure Quality** (15%)
- reversal occurring at meaningful structure, not mid-range chop

5. **Entry Efficiency** (10%)
- early/clean entry near the reversal zone
- penalize late entries

6. **Expected Move Sufficiency** (10%)
- enough runway toward the opposite structural side for a worthwhile long-hold trade

### Reversal gate
Add:
- `R100_REVERSAL_MIN_GATE = 58`

This is the PRIMARY gate and should be the easiest of the 3 R_100 engines to pass.

## T002 â€” Rebuild r100_breakout_engine with native scoring

This is the SECONDARY engine.

### Breakout thesis
Use breakout only when price escapes a meaningful structural boundary with genuine strength and holds/accepts beyond the break.

### Build a 6-component native breakout score
Implement a 0â€“100 weighted score with these dimensions:

1. **Boundary Pressure** (18%)
- repeated structural pressure at the edge before break

2. **Break Strength** (25%)
